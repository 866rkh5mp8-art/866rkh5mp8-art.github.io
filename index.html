Selected Work — Restaurants & Cafés
Meng Xi Ping · 孟曦萍
Photographer

Based in Hsinchu, moving to New York in 2026 for SVA MPS Fashion Photography. My work focuses on the atmosphere of dining spaces — the way light lands on a table, the quiet life between service, the textures that make a restaurant feel like somewhere worth staying. I shoot available light whenever possible. The result is images that feel like a memory — not a menu.

目前以新竹為基地，2026 年將前往紐約就讀 SVA MPS Fashion Photography。 我的作品聚焦在餐飲空間的氛圍——光線落在桌面的方式、服務空隙裡的靜謐、讓人想多待一會兒的質感。 我盡量以自然光拍攝。這樣的照片，感覺像一段記憶，不像一份菜單。
